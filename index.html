Safe Driving Guide

Sober Driving Guide

Make sure your headlights, taillights, and license plate light all work.
Make sure you and other passengers wear seat belts.
Do not play with your phone while driving.
Do not drink alcohol while driving.
If you drink alcohol, take a rest and take the DriveSobr test before driving again.
Do not drive if you are sleepy.
